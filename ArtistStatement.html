Noah Davis

Noah Davis is an aspiring artist who wishes to one day become an animator, character designer, and/or concept artist. Having always had an interest in drawing, Noah has recently made the jump from traditional art to digital. His biggest inspirations are the cartoons he grew up with (such as Gravity Falls, Over The Garden Wall, and Adventure Time), the natural world, and creatures from myth, legend, and folklore. His favorite things to draw are horrifying monsters, but he's recently been trying his hand at human characters as well! (which he arguably finds to be even more horrifying)

This is just an example of my work. To see more, check out my Gallery.
